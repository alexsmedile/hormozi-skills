# Skill: Lean Offer System (Idea → Offer → Pitch)

## Purpose
Turn any idea into a sellable offer, a clear pitch, and usable content — fast.

This skill combines:
- market selection
- offer creation
- value optimization
- pricing
- positioning
- hooks
- pitch
- sales structure

Goal: go from idea → something you can sell today.

---

## When to Use
Activate this skill when:
- you have an idea but no offer
- you have an offer but it doesn’t sell
- you need a full system, not isolated pieces
- you want speed and clarity

---

## Core Flows

### Short Mental Model
```text
Idea → Offer → Pitch

Full Flow

Idea → Market → Offer → Pitch → Content → Sales


⸻

Core Output

The assistant produces:
	•	a clear market
	•	a strong offer
	•	a structured pitch
	•	hooks for content
	•	a simple sales path

⸻

PART 1: IDEA → MARKET

1. Define the Market
	•	Who is this for?
	•	What pain do they feel now?
	•	Do they have money?
	•	Can you reach them easily?

Output:
	•	one-sentence avatar

⸻

2. Validate Demand

Check:
	•	urgency of pain
	•	existing demand
	•	willingness to pay

If weak → refine niche.

⸻

PART 2: MARKET → OFFER

3. Define Outcome
	•	what result do they want?
	•	make it specific and measurable

⸻

4. Map Obstacles

List:
	•	everything blocking the result

⸻

5. Build Solutions

Turn:
	•	obstacles → solutions → assets

⸻

6. Choose Delivery

Pick:
	•	DIY (scale)
	•	DWY (balance)
	•	DFY (premium)

Option:
	•	hybrid model

⸻

7. Reduce Time & Effort
	•	add quick wins
	•	remove steps
	•	add templates
	•	simplify execution

⸻

8. Build Value Stack
	•	core offer
	•	bonuses
	•	total value

⸻

9. Price the Offer
	•	anchor to value
	•	choose tier or single price
	•	justify clearly

⸻

PART 3: OFFER → PITCH

10. Position the Offer
	•	who it’s for
	•	what it does
	•	why it’s different

⸻

11. Create Hooks

Use:

WHO + RESULT + SPEED/EASE + OBJECTION REMOVAL

Generate multiple variations.

⸻

12. Handle Objections

Map:
	•	objection → belief → proof

⸻

13. Add Guarantee
	•	remove risk
	•	increase trust

⸻

14. Write Pitch

Include:
	•	problem
	•	outcome
	•	solution
	•	value stack
	•	price
	•	CTA

⸻

PART 4: PITCH → CONTENT

15. Generate Content Angles
	•	hooks
	•	pain points
	•	outcomes
	•	proof

⸻

16. Align Content with Offer

Each post:
	•	attracts the right audience
	•	connects to the offer

⸻

PART 5: CONTENT → SALES

17. Simple Sales Flow
	•	content → interest
	•	interest → offer
	•	offer → purchase

⸻

18. Add Upsells / Downsells
	•	increase revenue per customer

⸻

19. Build Offer Ladder
	•	entry
	•	core
	•	premium
